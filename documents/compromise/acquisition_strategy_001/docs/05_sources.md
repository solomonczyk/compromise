## Source notes

1. Poslovi Infostud: https://poslovi.infostud.com/  
Used for Serbian job-board market context, active-employer discovery and visible featured employers. Public page showed more than 15,000 open positions and more than 2,000 companies at review time.

2. Startuj Infostud: https://startuj.infostud.com/  
Used for early-career, student, first-job, internship and career-orientation ecosystem context.

3. EURES: https://eures.europa.eu/index_en  
Used for EU mobility context, jobseeker/employer matching, European Job Days and labour mobility research.

4. Serbian Business Registers Agency / APR: https://www.apr.gov.rs/  
Intended verification source for legal company existence before paid pilots.

5. Serbian Chamber of Commerce / PKS: https://pks.rs/  
Intended source for sector discovery and partnership exploration.

6. LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/  
Intended employer and recruiter discovery channel. Outreach must remain manual and relevant.

## Source use limitations

Public job boards are not vacancy-copying sources. Employer names from public pages are target-account leads, not proof of interest. Every employer must be verified through official website, official email domain, public company page or business register before paid engagement. Candidate communities are opt-in distribution channels, not data extraction sources.
