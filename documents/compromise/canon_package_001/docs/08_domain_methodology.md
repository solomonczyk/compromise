# Recruitment Domain Methodology

## Methodology purpose
The product's domain method is practical hiring facilitation. It improves clarity, safety and matching signals, but does not replace employer judgment or legal hiring obligations.

## Source of truth
- Candidate profile source of truth: candidate-entered data and explicit attachments.
- Employer vacancy source of truth: employer-entered vacancy details after moderation.
- Application source of truth: server-side submitted application record.
- Moderation source of truth: admin decision log with reason.
- Commercial source of truth: invoice, receipt, payment confirmation or accepted pilot agreement.

## Progression
The marketplace progresses from manual trust to semi-automated assistance:
1. Manual structured posting and moderation.
2. Manual candidate application flow.
3. Manual support and concierge shortlisting.
4. Advisory AI suggestions for text quality or risk classification.
5. Automated billing and advanced matching only after evidence and ADR.

## Assessment
Vacancy quality is assessed by clarity of role, compensation transparency or explanation, location/remote mode, requirements, contact safety and absence of deceptive claims. Candidate fit is assessed only as advisory matching signals, never as automatic acceptance or rejection.

## Quality criteria
A high-quality vacancy has concrete role, responsibilities, requirements, compensation or range policy, hiring location, contact path, employer identity and no unsafe payment request. A high-quality candidate profile has relevant experience, skills, languages, availability and contact preference.

## Content rules
Forbidden vacancy content includes illegal work, upfront payment from candidate, identity document collection before lawful need, misleading compensation, discriminatory criteria without lawful basis, suspicious off-platform urgency and contact-only posts without role details.

## Human review
Human review is required for employer verification during beta, suspicious vacancies, abuse reports, AI risk flags, payment disputes and candidate complaints about unsafe behavior.

## Authority boundaries
The platform may recommend, sort, label, moderate and facilitate. It may not legally guarantee employment, certify candidate truthfulness, provide migration advice, make final hiring decisions, or sell candidate data beyond explicit product scope.

## AI boundaries
AI may help rewrite a vacancy, summarize a candidate profile for the employer, detect missing fields, classify risk for moderator review and draft support replies. AI may not reject candidates, approve employers, make legal claims, or contact users without notification gate.

## Forbidden shortcuts
No fake vacancies for traction, no scraped personal data without lawful basis, no hidden candidate ranking, no bypass of moderation for paid employers, no manual spreadsheet data outside retention rules, no visual or domain PASS without operator review when human judgment is required.
