# decision_log

## D-001
- **id**: D-001
- **date**: 2026-06-11
- **decision**: Compromise is defined as a managed hiring marketplace, not a generic social network and not a resume parser only.
- **reason**: The user explicitly requested an HH.ru-like platform and end-to-end product governance.
- **status**: ACCEPTED

## D-002
- **id**: D-002
- **date**: 2026-06-11
- **decision**: Primary payer is employer; candidate core access remains free in MVP.
- **reason**: Marketplace liquidity requires candidate acquisition without payment friction.
- **status**: ACCEPTED

## D-003
- **id**: D-003
- **date**: 2026-06-11
- **decision**: MVP includes job posting, candidate profile, application, employer workspace and admin moderation only.
- **reason**: This is the smallest sellable hiring loop.
- **status**: ACCEPTED

## D-004
- **id**: D-004
- **date**: 2026-06-11
- **decision**: Paid pilot must precede any scalable paid advertising.
- **reason**: Marketing promises must be tied to product readiness and payment evidence.
- **status**: ACCEPTED

## D-005
- **id**: D-005
- **date**: 2026-06-11
- **decision**: AI Gateway is optional-advisory in MVP and forbidden from authoritative hiring or rejection decisions.
- **reason**: Recruitment decisions require fairness, explainability and human accountability.
- **status**: ACCEPTED
